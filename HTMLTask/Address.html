The tag defines the contact information for the author/owner of a document or an article.

The contact information can be an email address, URL, physical address, phone number, social media handle, etc.
        
The text in the element usually renders in italic, and browsers will always add a line break before and after the 
 element.
Ex:-
Written by Rudolph Barik.
Visit us at:
Gmail.com
Baripada, Odisha
India
